    Getting started

    • Place: choose a component on the left, then click the canvas.
    • Wire: drag from an output pin (right) to an input pin (left).
    • Move: drag any component body to reposition it.
    • Flip a switch: click an Input Switch to toggle it on/off.
    • Select: click a gate or wire; drag an empty area to box-select. Shift+click adds.
    • Delete: select, then press Delete.
    • Undo / redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y. Duplicate: Ctrl+D.
    • Rename: right-click a gate (or double-click it) to add a label.
    • Pan: drag with the middle mouse, hold Space, or two-finger scroll.
    • Zoom: pinch, or hold Ctrl and scroll.
    • Amber pin: an unconnected input — it reads as logic-low.
